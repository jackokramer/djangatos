Who we Are

Djangatos is THE band to catch in Silicon Valley, dazzling audiences from the SF Peninsula to the Santa Cruz coast. Featuring a unique blend of Hot Club jazz, Tin Pan Alley classics, Americana, and manouche swing originals, the trio is the perfect match for sipping wine or hitting the dance floor.
